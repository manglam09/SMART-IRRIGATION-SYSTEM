Smart Irrigation System

What is a Smart Irrigation System?

Smart irrigation is an advanced system that optimizes water usage by using real-time data from sensors, weather forecasts, and soil conditions. It helps farmers automate and manage irrigation efficiently, reducing water waste and improving crop yields.

How Does It Work?

The system integrates IoT (Internet of Things) technology, sensors, and automated controllers. Here's how it operates:

  • Soil moisture sensors measure the water content in the soil.
  • Weather data is collected to predict rainfall and avoid unnecessary irrigation.
  • Automated valves and pumps control the water supply based on real-time data.
  • Farmers can monitor and control the system remotely via a mobile app or website.

Benefits of Smart Irrigation

  • Reduces water consumption by up to 50%.
  • Increases crop productivity and health.
  • Minimizes manual labor and reduces costs.
  • Enhances sustainability by reducing water wastage.
